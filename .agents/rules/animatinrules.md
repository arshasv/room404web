# Animation Rules

## Rule 1 — Animations Must Support Atmosphere
Every animation should enhance suspense and immersion.

---

## Rule 2 — Avoid Excessive Effects
Too many effects can reduce performance and overwhelm users.

### Avoid
- Excessive flashing
- Overly fast transitions
- Distracting particle spam

---

## Rule 3 — Maintain Smoothness
Animations must remain smooth across devices.

### Performance Targets
- Stable frame rates
- GPU acceleration
- Reduced lag

---

## Rule 4 — Use Subtle Motion
Horror effects should feel atmospheric rather than chaotic.
