# ROOM 404 — Screenshot Analysis

## 1. Color Palette

The screenshot uses a **very dark, cinematic horror palette** with strong contrast.

### Dominant Colors
- **Deep blacks / charcoal shadows** → most of the environment is intentionally underlit.
- **Dark blue-gray tones** → walls, ambient lighting, and shadow areas.
- **Warm brown wood tones** → furniture and floor add realism and contrast.
- **Blood red accents** → used heavily for:
  - the “404” title
  - the active “CONTINUE” button
  - paint/slash effects
- **Dirty off-white / bone textures** → used for menu text and “ROOM”.

### Overall Mood
- High contrast between darkness and red highlights.
- Feels oppressive and claustrophobic.
- Strong “danger” signaling through the red UI.

# 2. UI Style

The UI is heavily inspired by **indie survival horror games**.

## Fonts
- Rough, distressed, hand-painted horror typography.
- Uneven edges with grunge texture.
- Looks intentionally imperfect and scratched.

## Buttons / Menu
Vertical menu aligned on the right side:
- CONTINUE
- NEW GAME
- OPTIONS
- EXIT

## Button Style
“CONTINUE” is highlighted with:
- a red paint-brush stroke background
- rough splatter edges
- bold contrast

## Visual Effects
The UI avoids futuristic HUD styles and instead uses:
- shadow overlays
- vignette darkness
- silhouette framing
- horror brush textures

## Borders / Frames
Screen edges contain irregular black torn-shadow shapes resembling:
- ink splatter
- monster shadows
- corrupted film overlays

This creates a psychological unease effect.

# 3. “ROOM 404” Logo / Title Style

The title design is aggressive and memorable.

## “ROOM”
- Large uppercase letters.
- Dirty chalk/concrete texture.
- Off-white / dusty gray color.
- Slight dripping/grain effect.

## “404”
- Huge blood-red typography.
- Thick brush-painted style.
- Aggressive texture featuring:
  - scratches
  - smears
  - dripping blood effect

## Composition
- “ROOM” stacked above “404”.
- Asymmetrical and intentionally messy.
- Strong indie horror branding.

## Impression
The logo communicates:
- danger

- forbidden space
